Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

At ChatCard, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data, what we collect, and how we keep your information secure. Our goal is to keep things simple, transparent, and respectful of your privacy.

Accounts & Authentication

We do not store passwords, emails, or any identity-related information.

You do not need a Google, OpenAI, or third-party account to use ChatCard.

What Data We Collect

ChatCard allows users to paste their own ChatGPT memory to generate a shareable card. Here’s how we handle that input:

  • The data you paste (e.g., your ChatGPT memory or profile) is processed locally in your browser and optionally sent to our backend for card generation.
  • No data is collected automatically. Everything you submit is voluntary and under your control.

Data Storage

  • We do not permanently store the content you paste unless you explicitly submit it to be saved.
  • Any stored data (such as public ChatCards) is tied to a unique link—not to your name or identity.
  • We do not track users or link data across sessions.

Analytics

  • We use privacy-focused analytics to improve ChatCard, but analytics are anonymized and do not contain personal data.
  • We do not track IP addresses or create user profiles.
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes.

Data Sharing

  • We do not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties. The data you paste remains private unless you choose to generate and share a public ChatCard link.

Security

  • While we do not store sensitive personal information, we still take security seriously.
  • Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Generated cards are stored securely and only accessible via unguessable URLs.
  • We implement regular security audits and update dependencies frequently.

Data Deletion & User Control

  • You remain in full control of your data.
  • You can delete your generated ChatCard at any time using the delete option.
  • No user accounts = no stored profile to erase—we don’t hold anything about you beyond what you explicitly submit.

Your Rights

  • Request access to any data you submitted
  • Request deletion of your generated card
  • Lodge complaints with your local data authority
  • We fully support your right to privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted clearly on our site with a revision date.
