About Us

Our Mission

Chatcard is a personality layer built on top of your AI chats. It helps you reflect on who you are, understand your communication style, and share your vibe with others, all by analyzing your conversations with AI. Whether you're building a personal brand or just curious about yourself, chatcard turns your digital trail into a beautiful, insightful card.

Why I Started

I built ChatCard because I realized how much of my personality shows up in my AI conversations - thoughts, emotions, goals, and even humor. Yet, no one was tapping into this raw, real data to help people understand themselves better. Social media is curated. Résumés are formal. But Chatcard shows your real personality based on how you actually think and talk.

Built for Transparency

Chatcard doesn’t require login or authentication directly to your AI accounts. You paste your exported ChatGPT (or other AI) memory, and we show you what it reveals about you. There’s no hidden tracking, just insights. I'm committed to keeping it simple, honest, and user-first.
