About Us

Sunpost is a quiet place to send letters, like postcards from your mind.

Sometimes you feel something you can’t explain out loud. Sometimes you want to tell someone how you’re really doing, or just hold onto a moment that feels too small for a journal but too big to forget. Sunpost lets you write those thoughts and make sure it's private. To yourself. To a friend. To no one in particular.

There are no likes, no noise, no pressure to be interesting. The receiver has to use their password to unlock your letter.

Keep your letters, send them, or come back to them later. Like folded notes in a shoebox, they’ll be waiting.
