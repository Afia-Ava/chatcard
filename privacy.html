Privacy Policy

Last updated: Aug 5, 2025

At Sunpost, your words are always yours. We believe in quiet spaces. That means:

We don’t collect personal data.
If you send a letter to someone’s email, we use their email address only to deliver that letter. We don’t sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. Ever.

Letters are private by default.
Letters you write stay private unless you choose to send them.
If you archive a letter, it’s stored only for you. No one else can see it.

We don’t track you.
No ads. No tracking across the web. No cookies that follow you around.
